AQUASENTRY // EXECUTIVE REPORT
Viksit Bharat Abhiyan - Buildathon Initiative
AquaSentry
Smart, low-cost, local water quality sentinel designed to provide communities with real-time environmental insights.
Core Objectives
Real-Time Monitoring
AquaSentry provides accurate, continuous water quality monitoring accessible through a simple dashboard interface.
pH & Turbidity Core chemical stability metrics.
Temperature & TDS Key environmental balance indicators.
IoT Integration Continuous, remote readings.
Alerts System Threshold-based SMS/email notifications.
Innovation
Low-Cost Scalability
Designed with locally available sensors and materials for easy manufacturing and deployment.
Impact
For Smarter Communities
Empowering rural and urban localities to identify contamination early and take sustainable action.
System Overview
Data-Driven Design
Live readings transmitted via WiFi module to a secure cloud endpoint, visualized on an adaptive dashboard.
Team Vision
Empowering Every Drop
From awareness to action — AquaSentry aims to be the bridge between environmental insight and community-driven response.
Next Steps
Towards a Cleaner Future
Pilot deployment across multiple localities, user feedback analysis, and partnership with municipal bodies for large-scale adoption.
