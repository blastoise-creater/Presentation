AQUASENTRY // EXECUTIVE REPORT
Viksit Bharat Abhiyan - Buildathon Initiative
AquaSentry
Smart, low-cost, local water quality sentinel: A critical infrastructure solution.
A self-powered device that measures water quality at handpumps and taps, delivering a clear consumer verdict and logging granular, precise data for institutional maintenance teams. Optimized for robust, rural deployment.
CHALLENGE: UNMONITORED INFRASTRUCTURE
Unseen contamination at source
Millions rely on untested handpumps and taps. Contaminated water causes disease, costs, and lost productivity.
HEALTH IMPERATIVE
Cholera, Typhoid, Diarrhea, and long-term illness.
TOXIC RISK
Fluoride, Arsenic, Heavy metals exposure in groundwater.
ECONOMIC DRAIN
Costs associated with reactive healthcare and productivity loss.
TESTING GAP
Expensive laboratory analysis and inconsistent check-up schedules.
STRATEGIC GAP ANALYSIS
Why monitoring is missing
High equipment cost, inconsistent power supply, lack of local expertise, and sensor maintenance gaps create systemic blind spots. AquaSentry closes these weaknesses.
  • **Prohibitive Cost:** High investment required for lab-grade monitoring systems.
  • **Autonomy Requirement:** Systems must operate reliably without external power infrastructure.
  • **Maintenance Specialization:** Need for simplified maintenance by local personnel.
  • **Data Integrity:** Challenge in securing consistent and timely data for action.
THE AQUASENTRY PROPOSITION: CORE VALUE
The System Guardian
Solar/battery-powered sensor node with simple color-coded verdicts and local data logging for maintenance teams.
  • **Local Transparency:** Immediate, clear verdict: Drinkable / Washing only / Unsafe.
  • **Economic Design:** Ultra-low-cost build (~₹3.5k) for scalable deployment.
  • **Auditable Logs:** Hourly data entries recorded on microSD with quick retrieval interface.
TECHNOLOGY ARCHITECTURE
Hardware & Power Autonomy
A modular assembly of commercial off-the-shelf components ensures ease of repair and low procurement cost. Key metrics measured by dedicated probes.
  • **Sensing Core:** TDS/pH probes housed in a flow-through enclosure.
  • **Processing Unit:** Arduino Nano/ESP32 for robust data processing.
  • **Power System:** 18650 battery, TP4056 charging module, MT3608 boost, with optional mini **Solar Panel** integration.
DATA FLOW & INTERPRETATION
Operational Logic
The embedded algorithm ensures immediate, reliable data translation, minimizing false positives and providing actionable intelligence.
01. DATA INTAKE
Raw analog sensor input (TDS/pH).
02. CALCULATION
Averaging, temperature compensation, and threshold check.
03. CONSUMER DISPLAY
OLED verdict (icon + text).
04. INSTITUTIONAL RECORD
Time-stamped CSV log to microSD storage.
SCALABILITY & INVESTMENT RETURN
Impact & Roadmap
A phased approach: pilot testing for local calibration, establishing a training framework, and large-scale deployment leveraging local artisans under the Atmanirbhar Bharat initiative.
TARGET DEPLOYMENT: 500 UNITS IN YEAR 2
Immediate Objectives: Prototype finalization, extensive community User Acceptance Testing (UAT), and securing seed funding for procurement and initial training workshops.
